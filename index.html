PLANNING STAT: A TOOL TO WRITE BETTER PERMITS

Amendments to the Planning and Environment Regulations 2015 have changed the way permits are written. Here is a tool to help you write better permits.

On 1 December 2024, amendments to the Planning and Environment Regulations 2015 (the Regulations) commenced. The amendments introduced new forms (4, 5, 7, 9, 10, 11 and 12) and changed the way responsible authorities document decisions to grant or refuse permissions under planning schemes.

The amended forms now include tables into which decision makers to specify the clause of the relevant permit requirement and matter for which a permit is required (the Permissions Table).

The logic of the changes are explained the recently re-written Writing Planning Permits (May 2023) guide (the Guide) issued by the DTP. The Guide, including the recent changes, were prepared by Equipe with Helen Gibson AM and Peter Allen PSM for DTP.

Equipe have developed a tool to help you easily build a Permissions Table. We hope it helps.
